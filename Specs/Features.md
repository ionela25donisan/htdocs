# Features

## Invoices

- Create and manage invoices for utilities, services, etc.
- Track payment status.

## Rents

- Record rent payments.
- Generate rent statements.

## Maintenance

- Schedule and track maintenance tasks.
- Assign to tenants or staff.

## Documents

- Store and organize important documents (leases, contracts, etc.).
- Search and retrieve documents.

## Tenants

- Manage tenant information.
- Track lease agreements.
